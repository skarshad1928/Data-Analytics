# 🏥 CareSight: Healthcare Cost & Readmission Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

CareSight is a data analytics project focused on improving hospital efficiency by analyzing patient readmission patterns and associated treatment costs. The system combines predictive analytics with financial insights to identify high-risk patient groups and cost drivers.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Predict **patient readmission risk** using historical clinical data
* Identify **cost anomalies** and operational inefficiencies
* Quantify the **financial impact of readmissions**
* Support **data-driven decision-making** for healthcare management

---

## 📊 Dataset

* Source: Public healthcare datasets (e.g., UCI Diabetes Readmission Dataset)
* Contains:

  * Patient demographics
  * Admission and discharge records
  * Medical conditions and treatments
  * Billing and cost-related features

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

* **Languages:** Python, SQL
* **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, XGBoost
* **Visualization:** Tableau / Power BI
* **Analysis:** Statistical modeling and clustering

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 1. Data Preprocessing

* Handled missing and inconsistent medical records
* Encoded categorical variables for modeling
* Engineered features related to patient history and visits

### 2. Readmission Risk Prediction

* Built classification models to predict 30-day readmissions
* Identified key factors influencing patient return rates

### 3. Patient Segmentation

* Applied clustering techniques to group patients by risk and cost
* Identified high-risk, high-cost patient segments

### 4. Cost & Anomaly Analysis

* Analyzed treatment costs across segments
* Detected unusual billing patterns and outliers

### 5. Interactive Dashboard

* Visualized:

  * Readmission rates
  * Cost distribution
  * Risk segmentation
* Enabled stakeholders to explore trends and patterns

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Identified patient groups with high readmission probability
* Highlighted major cost drivers linked to repeat admissions
* Detected anomalies indicating potential inefficiencies

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

* Supports **reduction in avoidable readmissions**
* Improves **cost management and resource allocation**
* Enables **targeted intervention strategies** for high-risk patients
